Current Research

TRACE-UMIA / TRACE-MultiStat

My main research direction right now is TRACE-UMIA. It is not an unlearning method. It is an audit method. The idea is to check whether approximate unlearning still leaves internal traces of forget samples inside transformer text classifiers.

I compare the model before and after unlearning, extract token-level hidden representation drift, and use simple multi-statistic features with logistic regression to audit whether forget samples move differently from retain and same-source unseen samples.

The safest claim from my current experiments is that internal representation based audits can detect forget-specific traces that output-only audits may miss or under-detect, especially for small targeted forget sets.
