Current Research

TRACE-UMIA

My main research direction right now is TRACE-UMIA. It is an machine unlearning audit method. The idea is to check whether approximate unlearning leaves internal traces of forget samples inside transformer text classifiers, especially when the forget sample is small and output score based auditing methods miss the signal.
